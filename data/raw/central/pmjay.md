# PM-JAY — Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

## Who may qualify

Families identified as economically vulnerable in the Socio-Economic Caste Census 2011 (SECC) are covered. Unorganised workers registered on e-Shram may also qualify under the AB PM-JAY expansion for unorganised sector workers. The scheme is fully portable across all empanelled hospitals in India, including in Telangana and Maharashtra.

## Documents needed

- Aadhaar card
- e-Shram UAN or SECC inclusion proof
- Ayushman card (generated after eligibility verification)
- Ration card (if applying under SECC route)

## What benefit

The scheme provides cashless hospitalisation cover of up to rupees five lakh per family per year at any empanelled government or private hospital. The cover includes pre-hospitalisation, hospitalisation, and post-hospitalisation expenses for over 1,900 medical procedures. There is no cap on family size.

## How to access (Bihar to Hyderabad corridor)

1. Check eligibility at pmjay.gov.in or call 14555 (toll-free).
2. Visit an empanelled hospital in Hyderabad with your Aadhaar and e-Shram card.
3. The hospital's Ayushman Mitra will verify eligibility and generate your Ayushman card on the spot.
4. You may also generate your Ayushman card at the nearest CSC using Aadhaar and e-Shram UAN.
5. Present the Ayushman card at any empanelled hospital for cashless treatment. No cash deposit is needed.

## What to do if denied

If the hospital denies cashless treatment, ask for a written reason and escalate to the State Health Agency helpline for Telangana. You may also call the national PM-JAY helpline at 14555. If your name is not in the SECC database, apply through the e-Shram route at a CSC with your UAN.
