# e-Shram (Unorganised Workers National Database)

## Who may qualify

Any worker in the unorganised sector aged 16 to 59 years who is not a member of EPFO or ESIC may register. This includes construction labourers, daily-wage workers, domestic workers, street vendors, and agricultural workers who have migrated to another state for work.

## Documents needed

- Aadhaar card (mandatory)
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
- Bank account number (Jan Dhan or any savings account)
- Self-declaration of occupation and income

## What benefit

Registration generates a permanent 12-digit Universal Account Number (UAN) on the e-Shram card. This UAN is portable across all states and serves as the gateway to PM Suraksha Bima Yojana (accident insurance), PM-JAY (health cover), and PM Shram Yogi Maandhan (pension). Registered workers may receive priority access to government welfare during disasters and emergencies.

## How to access (Bihar to Hyderabad corridor)

1. Visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) in Hyderabad or use the self-registration portal at eshram.gov.in.
2. Provide Aadhaar number and authenticate via OTP on the linked mobile number.
3. Enter occupation details and bank account number.
4. Download or collect the e-Shram card with your UAN immediately after registration.
5. Registration is free. No agent fee is required.

## What to do if denied

If OTP does not arrive, visit the nearest CSC with your Aadhaar card in person. If your Aadhaar mobile link is outdated, update it at an Aadhaar Seva Kendra first. For unresolved issues, call the e-Shram helpline at 14434 (toll-free, available in Hindi and other languages).
