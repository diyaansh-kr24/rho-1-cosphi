# PM Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)

## Who may qualify

Any bank account holder aged 18 to 70 years with a Jan Dhan or savings account linked to Aadhaar may enrol. Workers registered on e-Shram are automatically eligible. The scheme covers accidental death and permanent disability.

## Documents needed

- Aadhaar card
- Jan Dhan or savings bank account number
- Mobile number linked to the bank account
- e-Shram UAN (if enrolling through the e-Shram channel)

## What benefit

The scheme provides accident insurance cover of rupees two lakh for accidental death or permanent total disability. It provides rupees one lakh for permanent partial disability. The annual premium is rupees twenty only, auto-debited from the linked bank account in May each year. The cover runs from 1 June to 31 May.

## How to access (Bihar to Hyderabad corridor)

1. Complete e-Shram registration first to obtain a UAN.
2. Visit your Jan Dhan bank branch in Hyderabad or use the bank's mobile banking app.
3. Request enrolment in PMSBY by providing your Aadhaar and bank account details.
4. Confirm the auto-debit consent for the annual premium of rupees twenty.
5. You may also enrol at any CSC in Hyderabad after completing e-Shram registration.

## What to do if denied

If your bank does not enrol you, visit the branch manager with your Aadhaar and e-Shram card. If the auto-debit fails due to insufficient balance, ensure at least rupees twenty is in the account by 25 May. For disputes, call the PMSBY helpline through your bank or visit the nearest District Labour Office.
