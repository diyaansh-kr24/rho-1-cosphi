# PM Shram Yogi Maandhan (PM-SYM)

## Who may qualify

Unorganised workers aged 18 to 40 years with a monthly income of rupees fifteen thousand or less may enrol. The worker must not be covered by EPFO, ESIC, or NPS, and must have a Jan Dhan or savings bank account linked to Aadhaar. Workers registered on e-Shram are eligible to apply.

## Documents needed

- Aadhaar card
- Jan Dhan or savings bank account number and IFSC code
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
- e-Shram UAN (recommended for faster verification)
- Self-declaration of income below rupees fifteen thousand per month

## What benefit

The scheme guarantees a minimum pension of rupees three thousand per month after the age of 60. The contribution is matched equally by the Central Government. Monthly contribution ranges from rupees fifty-five (if you join at age 18) to rupees two hundred (if you join at age 40). Contributions are auto-debited from the linked bank account.

## How to access (Bihar to Hyderabad corridor)

1. Complete e-Shram registration first.
2. Visit the nearest CSC in Hyderabad with your Aadhaar, bank passbook, and mobile phone.
3. The CSC operator will register you on the maandhan.in portal and set up the monthly auto-debit.
4. You will receive a Shram Yogi Maandhan card with your pension account number.
5. You may also self-enrol on the maandhan.in portal using your Aadhaar and bank details.

## What to do if denied

If your income is considered above the threshold, provide wage slips or a self-declaration letter. If the CSC cannot process your application, visit the nearest District Labour Office. For helpline support, call 14434 or 1800-267-6888 (toll-free).
