# One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) — Telangana

## Who may qualify

Any National Food Security Act (NFSA) beneficiary who holds a valid ration card issued in Bihar may use it at any Fair Price Shop (FPS) in Telangana under the ONORC portability scheme. The card must be active and linked to Aadhaar. Migrant workers from Bihar currently working in Hyderabad or elsewhere in Telangana are directly covered.

## Documents needed

- Bihar ration card (physical or digital copy)
- Aadhaar card of the cardholder
- Aadhaar-linked mobile number (for OTP authentication at the FPS machine)

## What benefit

The scheme allows you to collect your entitled subsidised food grains (rice and wheat at NFSA prices) from any Fair Price Shop in Telangana. You receive the same quantity entitled under your Bihar ration card. You do not need to surrender the Bihar card. The portability is available for both PHH (Priority Household) and AAY (Antyodaya Anna Yojana) card holders.

## How to access (Bihar to Hyderabad corridor)

1. Locate the nearest Fair Price Shop in Hyderabad using the Mera Ration app or the NFSA portal at nfsa.gov.in.
2. Visit the FPS with your Bihar ration card and Aadhaar card.
3. Provide your Aadhaar number at the ePoS machine and authenticate with your fingerprint or OTP.
4. Collect your entitled monthly food grains. No advance registration at a Telangana office is required.
5. If the FPS dealer refuses portability, escalate to the Telangana Civil Supplies district office.

## What to do if denied

If fingerprint authentication fails repeatedly, request OTP-based authentication using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. If the dealer claims the system does not show your entitlement, note the FPS dealer's ID and contact the Telangana Civil Supplies helpline or visit the district Civil Supplies office in Hyderabad with your ration card and Aadhaar.
