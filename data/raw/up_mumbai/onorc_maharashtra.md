# One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) — Maharashtra

## Who may qualify

Any National Food Security Act (NFSA) beneficiary who holds a valid ration card issued in Uttar Pradesh may use it at any Fair Price Shop (FPS) in Maharashtra under the ONORC portability scheme. The card must be active and linked to Aadhaar. Migrant workers from Uttar Pradesh currently working in Mumbai or elsewhere in Maharashtra are directly covered.

## Documents needed

- Uttar Pradesh ration card (physical or digital copy)
- Aadhaar card of the cardholder
- Aadhaar-linked mobile number (for OTP authentication at the FPS machine)

## What benefit

The scheme allows you to collect your entitled subsidised food grains (rice and wheat at NFSA prices) from any Fair Price Shop in Maharashtra. You receive the same quantity entitled under your Uttar Pradesh ration card. You do not need to surrender the UP card or transfer it. The portability applies to both PHH (Priority Household) and AAY (Antyodaya Anna Yojana) card holders.

## How to access (UP to Mumbai corridor)

1. Locate the nearest Fair Price Shop in Mumbai using the Mera Ration app or the NFSA portal at nfsa.gov.in.
2. Visit the FPS with your Uttar Pradesh ration card and Aadhaar card.
3. Provide your Aadhaar number at the ePoS machine and authenticate with your fingerprint or OTP.
4. Collect your entitled monthly food grains. No advance registration at a Maharashtra office is required.
5. If the FPS dealer refuses portability, escalate to the Maharashtra Civil Supplies district office in Mumbai.

## What to do if denied

If fingerprint authentication fails repeatedly, request OTP-based authentication using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. If the dealer claims the system does not show your entitlement, note the FPS dealer's ID and contact the Maharashtra Civil Supplies helpline or visit the district Civil Supplies office in Mumbai with your ration card and Aadhaar.
