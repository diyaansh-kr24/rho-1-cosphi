# Telangana Building and Other Construction Workers (BOCW) Board

## Who may qualify

Construction workers who have worked in Telangana for at least ninety days in the preceding twelve months may register with the Telangana BOCW Board. The board provides benefits such as accident compensation, education assistance, and housing loans to registered members.

## Documents needed

- Aadhaar card
- Proof of employment as a construction worker (wage slip, contractor certificate, or employer letter)
- Proof of ninety days of work in Telangana (attendance record or employer certificate)
- Bank account details

## What benefit

Registered members receive accident and death compensation, education scholarships for children, assistance for housing loans, and pension support. Benefits vary and are determined by the Telangana BOCW Board at the time of application.

## How to access

IMPORTANT: This scheme is administered exclusively by the Telangana Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. It is a state-specific board scheme and is not portable across states. Workers who registered with the Bihar BOCW Board cannot transfer their registration to Telangana. A new registration in Telangana requires fresh proof of employment in Telangana for ninety days. Contact the District Labour Office, Hyderabad, for registration and assistance.

## What to do if denied

If your application is denied, contact the District Labour Office in Hyderabad with the rejection notice. You may also approach the Assistant Commissioner of Labour for the relevant district. This system is separate from centrally administered schemes and requires direct engagement with state labour authorities.
